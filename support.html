Common questions

What age is KidOS designed for?

KidOS is designed for children ages 6–15.

Is KidOS a chore app, a rewards app, or a learning app?

No — it's the system underneath all three. KidOS focuses on daily habits, responsibility, and curiosity, not task completion, academic content, or a rewards marketplace.

How do rewards work?

Core daily activities (reading, thinking, curiosity, creativity, physical activity) are never paid — they're the point of the app, not a task to be compensated for. Only extra-effort tasks (like homework or practice) can earn points. Kids can propose rewards; a parent always reviews and approves them before they're redeemable.

Can my child use KidOS without me?

A child can start using KidOS on their own in a limited "Starter Mode." Full functionality — rewards, wallet spending, and multi-device sync — requires a parent to link their account. We designed it this way so a curious kid can try it immediately, while the features that matter (money, rewards, data) always stay under parent control.

Is my child's data safe? Do you show ads?

We don't show third-party ads to children, we don't sell family data, and we don't use behavioral tracking to target your child. See our Privacy Policy for the full detail on what we collect and why.

How do I review, correct, or delete my child's data?

Email support.kidos@gmail.com from the account holder's registered address and we'll help you review, correct, or permanently delete your family's data. See Delete Your Account for the full step-by-step process, or the "Parent Rights & Data Deletion" section of our Privacy Policy for detail.

What does KidOS cost?

KidOS has a free tier to get started. A premium plan adds AI-informed recommendations, deeper insights, and support for multiple children — pricing will be announced closer to launch and will vary by region.

When is KidOS launching?

We're still in development and validating the core experience with early families before a wider launch. Join the waitlist on the home page and we'll email you as soon as it's ready.
